The Swamp

Welcome to my swamp. When walking in the muddy swamp you will hear a noise coming from the trees. You will also notice that the noise is coming from big green eye from the trees. You should not close to the trees because the big green eyes belong to the big alligator. There's a haunted house in the middle of the swamp. When you walk by you will hear a great big "Boo!"
