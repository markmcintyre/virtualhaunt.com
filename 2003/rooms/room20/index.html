The Library

There was a haunted house that had its own library and its own graveyard. A witch peeked through she said I will pick a book with vampires. She found a book about vampires. She signed it out. A scarecrow went in the library said to the goblin who was working in the library do sell any books about dead ones. Oh! Yes I do! So he found a book of dead ones he signed it out. Then a mummy walked in the library the mummy said do you sell a book about dead people. Oh yes we do the mummy found a book of dead people. He signed it out. He opened the book. Boo!
