The Parlour

Welcome to the horrifying parlour. When you walk in, the ghost behind the counter will ask you if you want some kind of drink, like eyeball tea or a blood caesar. Say no! Watch out for the blood stained carpet! The witch is having tea with Frankenstein. Make sure she does not see you. Hurry! You better get out of here, the mummy is getting angry because you are making too much noise, and he can't pour the eyeball tea. So, out the door and into the darkness you go.
