The Back Hallway

As you dare to step into the dark hallway, walk with caution. Be very careful of the Danger Hole, no one knows what it holds. The walls are full of witches' belongings: brooms, capes, hats, cauldrons, and black widow spiders hiding in their webs. Be very silent, because if you are heard, they will all come to life. A tap, tap, tap coming from the door, you will hear. Do not go near! Just move on. Bewitching eyes on the painting will try to trick your steps. Do not touch the pumpkin plant head or surely you will be dead. Slowly walk up the wobbling narrow stairs, a ghost of previous souls will lead you up to your next room.
