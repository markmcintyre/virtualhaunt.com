Welcome

Welcome to the Virtual Haunted House, a project for elementary classrooms to share their creative artwork over the World Wide Web! Each haunted house is an interactive tour through a spooky haunted house, complete with sound effects and music (if your web browser supports it). Please feel free to explore the past haunted houses!
