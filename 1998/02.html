The Graveyard - By David
    It was the middle of the night in a graveyard and a bat was flying around. The bat was no ordinary bat, he was a magic bat who had a mind of a human and could turn into an eight foot lizard. He was flying around just having fun when he read a grave stone that said, 'Rest in Peace', and the bat hated peace! So the bat made himself turn into an eight foot lizard to break off the peace signs. The lizard's claws were as sharp as swords and his breath was as hot as lava and his eyes were so red that if you looked at them you would burn to a crisp. All night long he was breaking off the peace signs but before he got to finish breaking all the grave stones, the clock struck seven and the people came out to work. Seeing the people made him turn back into a bat, but when the people leave, from that night on he keeps on breaking peace signs in the graveyard. Watch your gravestones!
