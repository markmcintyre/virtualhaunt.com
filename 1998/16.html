The Attic - By Jessie
In the attic there are many
weird and scary things like
skeletons rattling, bats,
ghosts flying, eyeballs creeping
out from behind coffins and Dracula
talking to jack-o-lanterns
but the scariest thing of all is the witch
stirring her brew and saying this spell:

Bubble, bubble toil and trouble
Fire burn and cauldron bubble
Eye of newt and tail of fish
in you go, don't make trouble
A fillet of a fenny fish, ear of cat
and tail of dog. Double double
Toil and trouble fire burn and
cauldron bubble.
